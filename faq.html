Frequently Asked Questions

Project Turn Around

We will always strive to delivery the project as quickly as possible. However as each individual project is unique and requires its own process in post production this can take from 14 to 30 days from the day of shooting.

Project Proofing

Upon project completion in the edit you will receive a first draft of the agrees project. Depending on the delivery timeline this can be to clarify creative or artistic decisions incumbent to the final video. Any media inclusive of the first draft is not permitted for public consumption.

Edits and Revisions

Upon receipt of the finished and approved project it is recommended that you take up to 48hrs to watch and review the video. We advise you to this at different times in the day and at different locations to make sure you are definitely happy with the end result. At the end of the 48hr period you are able to send us a full page of edits, notes and changes that you wish to see implemented. The desired changes will be turned around within 7 days and the revised project will be transferred to you. Any further changes after the initial review session will incur a further charge at 15% of the agreed rate of the project (I.E / Agreed rate-£300 x0.15 = £45).

Project Storage

Upon Completion of the finished project (Including Revisions) the captured footage and material will be saved on hard storage for a maximum of 60 days after which it will be permanently removed. You will receive a reminder near the time of deletion and can decide if you wish to keep the footage in storage for use in further projects or not. This need not apply to clients on service retainers.

Project Sharing and Distribution

We hope that you are thoroughly satisfied with you finished project and that you are proud to share it across and all social media platforms. We would kindly ask that you credit the team using the handle @Ronin347Media.
