Our Story

Hi, I'm Dan, I run Ronin347.

I grew up watching martial arts and old Kurosawa films and I used to think they were the absolute bees knees. This sparked my passion for cinematography.

I have spent the majority of my life honing my craft of lifting heavy stuff and putting it back down again, only to then go and lift it up again. I took this to the next level and qualified as a personal trainer.

I also attended the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland, making me a classically trained actor.

After making some moderate success in acting, I decided to take my personal training experience, my love for action cinematography and blend it all together to create bespoke and unique cinematic experiences.

I'm always looking to challenge myself (haven't been beaten yet), so if you have a project you think could be tough, let's size it up together.
